Cursed Artifacts

A sneak peek at what awaits inside

The Devil's Rocking Chair

From Ed & Lorraine Warren's notorious "The Devil Made Me Do It" case that inspired The Conjuring 3, this is the actual demonic throne the "Beast" claimed as its own.

Peggy The Doll

Touted as one of the most haunted dolls in the world, Peggy has caused countless nosebleeds, fainting, and even a heart attack. Guests are warned beforehand against looking her directly in her eyes.

The Dybbuk Box

Known as "The Most Haunted Object in the World", the Dybbuk Box has been claimed to have been responsible for multiple deaths, a stroke, and cursing famed musician Post Malone.

Dr. Death's Van

The actual death van of Dr. Jack Kevorkian, where he carried out numerous assisted suicides. The van has been a hotspot of paranormal activity, particularly for females who have fainted.

Demon House

In what was called the most authenticated case of demonic possession in America, this exhibit contains the actual "staircase to hell", as well as the dirt and ritual items discovered by police.

Bobby Mackey's Music World

Visit the infamous haunted honky tonk that was the subject of the first ever Ghost Adventures episode. Visit the "Hell's Gate" well where a demonic sacrifice unleashed this establishment to pure hell.

WHAT SHOULD I BRING TO THE TOUR?

+

Please bring a valid photo ID, your booking confirmation, and wear comfortable walking shoes. For the Live Investigation experience, we provide all paranormal investigation equipment. Photography is not permitted inside the museum to respect the artifacts and other guests.

IS THERE AN AGE RESTRICTION?

+

Yes, all guests must be 12 years or older to enter The Haunted Museum. Guests under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian. The Live Investigation experience is restricted to guests 18 and older due to the intense nature of the activity.

CAN I GET A REFUND IF I CANCEL?

+

Full refunds are available for cancellations made at least 48 hours before your scheduled experience. Cancellations within 48 hours may be eligible for a partial refund or rescheduling. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule.
